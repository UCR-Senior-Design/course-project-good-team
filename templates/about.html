Friendify is a feature that will add to your Spotify experience. This feature aims to expand social interactions between users by adding attributes that will give you interaction between friends.

  • View your top tracks and top artists
  • Access your listening statistics
  • Request random recommendations
  • Get personal recommendations based on friends' playlists

Spotify users will use our webpage to log into their respective accounts. Through user log in authentication and validation we then use Spotify users' statistics to personalize their experience.
